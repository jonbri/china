Hong Kong Dim Sum

A famous Hong Kong Dim Sum place with a long history. Where the locals go.

Hong Kong Dim Sum tastes much different than westernized Dim Sum...meaning that none of us really enjoyed it!

This is where I was also introduced to the ritual of having to clean your own utensils before eating.
It's an established part of the process...you are given a special bowl which you fill with hot soup that you douse your utensils in for cleaning purposes.
Before the food is served, the dirty bowl is taken away as you sip fresh tea (from the same bowl as the tea you just used to clean with!)

This cleaning ritual seems to be limited to just the province that Hong Kong and Shenzhen are located in.

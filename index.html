Taiyuen

Literally right next to Tracy's parents apartment is an army base.
That means you wake up early each morning to Chinese nationalistic music blaring. And at lunch, and supper time.
It's pretty interesting to hear, much more beautiful than American rigid military music. Not sure I'd want to hear it every day though

Taiyuen is a special city, but the first thing a forigner learns is the smell. And the alley that you must walk down to get out of the neighborhood has been deemed "poop alley".
After a good rain, which will stir up the sewage, the smell is so bad that I was gagging and almost puked as I walked down the street. Somehow I got used to it.

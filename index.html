China September 2014

In early September 2014 Me and Tracy went to China and Japan!

This was my first time in China, but I had done some preparation.
I had learned the very basics of mandarin chinese and had eaten plenty of "authentic" chinese food in America

The Plane Ride wasn't too bad. Turns out the international flights have little monitors on the back of the seats so you can play games and goof off! I planned to do all sorts of reading and work, but basically got nothing done!
